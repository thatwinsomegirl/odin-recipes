Basic Lousiana Rice

This recipe makes the most delightful accompaniment to Red Beans and Rice, Cajun Sheet Pan Sausage with Veggies, or any other of your favorite Cajun Dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups long-grain white rice, such as Jasmine
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1-2 pinches of salt

Steps:

  1. Put the olive oil and onions into a medium saucepan over medium heat, sweating the onions until they are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the rice to the pan and stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Next, add the chicken stock, bay leaf and salt, and bring to a boil.
  4. Cover the pan with a lid, reduce heat to low, and cook for 18 minutes.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat, fluff rice with a fork, and serve.
