Cajun Sheet Pan Sausage with Veggies

This is a favorite recipe of my family and a brilliant way to utilize the bounty from your summer garden. It's divine and a complete meal on it's own, but to make it even more filling or to stretch further for company, try pairing with the Basic Louisiana Rice recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz. Andouille sausage, sliced into 1/2" rounds
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into 1/2" half-rounds
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced into 1/2" half-rounds
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped into 1" pieces
  • 1 sweet yellow onion, chopped into 1" pieces
  • 12 oz. okra, sliced into 1/2" rounds
  • 2 Tablespoons avocado oil, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the prepared sausage and vegetables with 1 Tablespoon of oil and seasonings until well coated.
  3. Drizzle remaining 1 Tablespoon of oil onto a half sheet pan and spread out seasoned sausage and veggies into an even layer.
  4. Roast in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, until veggies are soft and sausage has a bit of golden color.
  5. Serve warm plain, or over rice.
